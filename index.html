Frequently Asked
Questions.

The mailbox is IoT-enabled, meaning it connects directly to your home Wi-Fi network. This internet connectivity allows it to send instant updates and sync data with your cloud portal completely remotely.

No extra app downloads required! The system features Instant Telegram Notifications. You will receive a real-time message directly on your Telegram app the exact moment mail or a package is delivered.

The mailbox uses RFID-Based Secure Access. You will receive authorized RFID key fobs or cards with your purchase. Simply tap your key card against the reader to instantly unlock the secure door.

We built this with high-security standards. The mailbox features built-in Tamper Detection. If someone tries to force the door open, shake, or unauthorizedly break into the mailbox, the internal sensors will instantly trigger an emergency alert to your phone.

Yes. The system maintains continuous Timestamped Access Logs. Every single time the mailbox door is unlocked or opened, the exact date and time are securely recorded, giving you a transparent audit trail of your deliveries.

The mailbox is designed with a secure, one-way drop slot or a dedicated carrier access mechanism. Mail carriers can easily drop mail or small parcels in, but the design prevents anyone from reaching back inside to fish them out. Only you hold the RFID key to unlock the main compartment.

The mailbox features an internal backup battery system to keep the physical locking mechanism and tamper sensors active. If the Wi-Fi disconnects, the system logs all timestamped events locally and syncs them to your history the moment the connection is restored.
