01 / POWER

Self-Charging Solar Panel

A top-mounted monocrystalline solar strip harvests sunlight to power the internal 10,000mAh battery. Completely wire-free, self-sustaining operation that works even on overcast days.

02 / VISIBILITY

Ambient LED Status Bar

A striking horizontal blue light bar across the front communicates mailbox status. Pulsing cyan means mail is inside, blinking white indicates a courier is scanning, and amber alerts warn you of tamper attempts.

03 / INTERACTION

OLED Courier Interface

A side-mounted crisp OLED display guides delivery drivers. Shows confirmation prompts like "Package Registered," battery indicators, and Wi-Fi connection strength.

04 / INTEGRITY

Encrypted Electronic Lock

Our heavy-duty steel lock pin holds the drop lid firmly secure. Auto-unlocks via banking-grade encrypted Bluetooth as you approach with your mobile phone, keeping mail safe from porch pirates.

05 / STRUCTURE

Reinforced Mounting

Machined from aerospace-grade structural aluminum. The universal mount supports standard driveway mailbox posts and clean, flush brick-wall installations.
